Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Basic websites: 1-2 working days
Standard websites: 3-4 working days
Premium websites: 5-7 working days

Do I need to purchase hosting separately?

Hosting is included free with the Basic and Standard plans. For Premium, hosting is available as an add-on at €4/month with unlimited bandwidth and a free SSL certificate.

Can I update my website content myself?

No, website content can only be updated by our team. Changes are priced per plan: Basic — 10 free changes, then €2/change; Standard — €3 per change; Premium — €7 per change. A "change" means one content update (text, image, color, etc.). Major structural changes may count as multiple changes.

Do you provide SEO services?

Yes, all our packages include basic SEO optimization.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments by card, bank transfer, and cryptocurrency. A 20% deposit is required to start the project, with the balance due upon completion.

Can I see examples of your work?

Yes! We have example websites for each package type. Check out the example section above each package to see what kind of website you'll get.
